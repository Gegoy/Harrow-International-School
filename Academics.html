Our Academic Programs

Where Knowledge Meets Curiosity and Excellence

Academic Excellence at Harrow

At Harrow International School, we offer a comprehensive and personalized academic journey that ensures each student reaches their full potential. With a strong focus on both traditional and innovative approaches, our curriculum is designed to inspire critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for learning.

Primary School

Our Primary School program lays a strong foundation for lifelong learning through a focus on core subjects such as literacy, mathematics, and science, while developing creativity and curiosity in young minds.

Secondary School

In Secondary School, students engage in a variety of subjects, preparing them for higher education. We emphasize independent learning, collaboration, and critical thinking skills.

Sixth Form

Our Sixth Form program provides specialization pathways, including A-Levels and International Baccalaureate, preparing students for university and beyond, with individual support and mentorship.

STEM Education

STEM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) are cornerstones of our curriculum, providing hands-on experiences and developing problem-solving skills needed for the modern world.

Arts & Humanities

The Arts and Humanities play an essential role in our curriculum, encouraging creative expression through music, art, drama, and literature, as well as a deep understanding of history and culture.

Global Programs

We offer opportunities for students to engage in global learning programs, including international exchanges and study abroad experiences, broadening their perspectives and preparing them for a global future.
