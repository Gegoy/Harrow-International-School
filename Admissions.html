We are excited to welcome new students to Harrow International School. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that every prospective student finds the right path to success.

Step 1: Inquiry & Submission

Complete our online inquiry form to express your interest and submit the application form along with the required documents.

Step 2: Assessment

All applicants will undergo an assessment appropriate for their age group, which may include academic tests, interviews, or a portfolio review.

Step 3: Offer & Acceptance

Successful candidates will receive an offer letter. Once the offer is accepted, the enrollment procedures will begin.

Step 4: Orientation

New students and families will be invited to an orientation program to ensure a smooth transition into the school.

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Copy of Birth Certificate or Passport
  • Previous School Records (if applicable)
  • Recommendation Letter (if applicable)
  • Student Photo (Passport Size)
