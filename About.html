About Us

Discover the Legacy, Vision, and Values that Define Us

History

Founded more than a century ago, Harrow International School is more than just an educational institution. We are a close-knit community committed to nurturing the leaders and innovators of the future. Our school has a proud history of academic excellence, character development, and fostering global citizens ready to make a positive impact on the world.

What do we envision?

We envision a world where education empowers individuals to surpass limits, challenge norms, and lead with integrity. Our mission is to cultivate minds that are creative, resilient, and ready to face the challenges of the future.

What do we have?

At the core of Harrow International School are the values of excellence, respect, and community. We believe in the importance of fostering curiosity, promoting cultural diversity, and building character in all our students. These core principles shape the future of every student who passes through our school.
